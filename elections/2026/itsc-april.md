This is a record of the 2026 ITSC member elections.

- Cole Kennedy (TestifySec)
- Trishank Kuppusamy (Datadog)

## Candidates

unfortunately disqualifies him:

- Andrew McNamara (RedHat)

Given that there are as many seats as candidates, no votes were held.
